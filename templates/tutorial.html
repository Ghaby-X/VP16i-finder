Getting started with VP16i-finder

For prediction of single molecule

Step 1: go to upload page

step 2: enter your canonical smile into the text box

step 3: select your model to use

step 4: click on submit button

step 5: Result

For prediction of list of molecules

Note: the uploaded document should strictly be csv which contains only your smiles. There should be no headers in your csv file. Use the example.csv file provided as reference

step 1: Click on upload, and select your csv file from the popup

step 2: select the model to use

step 3: click on submit button

step 4: Results
